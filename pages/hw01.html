English Grammar Cheat Sheet

Three BIG Verbs

Pronoun To BE (Status) To DO (Action) To HAVE (Possession)
He / She / It i$ doe$ ha$
You / We / They are do have
I am

Examples:
• He i$ a teacher.
• She doe$ her exercise.
• It ha$ some free time.

PRESENT Tense Timeline

Continuous
am / i$ / are
in progress
Simple
do / doe$
right now/regularly
Perfect
have / ha$
all done


Statement Structure

For standard statements: Subject + Verb + Object.

Question structure

with question words:
• What/Where/How + DO + you/we/they/I + verb?
• What/Where/How + DOE$ + he/she/it + verb?

without question words:
• DO + you/we/they/I + verb?
• DOE$ + he/she/it + verb?
